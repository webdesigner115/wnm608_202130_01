White Peach Mouse Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 White peach
  • 10g Gelatin
  • 200ml Milk
  • 100ml Heavy Cream
  • 15g Sugar
  • 500g White Chocolate
  • 1 tbsp Strawberry Powder

Instructions

  • Step1: Peel or slice a peach then dice it. Put gelatin into ice water(8 minutes)

  • Step2: Put milk, light cream and sugar into a pot, then boil them until slightly smoky. Add gelatin into the pot and stir it until smooth

  • Step3: Pour the liquid into the moulds and add peach dices. Put it into fridge and frozen it six hours

  • Step4: Melt the white chocolate in a pot until smooth

  • Step5: Carefully take the mousse cakes out of the moulds and coat the mousse cakes with melted white chocolate

  • Step6: Get your serving dish and dust with strawberry powder

  • Step7: Enjoy your dessert

Sweet Potato Tempura

Ingredients

  • 1 Sweet potato/Purple Sweet Potato
  • 100g Tempura batter Mix Powder
  • 60g Bread Crumbs
  • 160ml Water

Instructions

  • Step1: slice sweet potato thinly and evenly. Mix the tempura batter mix powder with water.

  • Step2: heat the oil and coat sweet potatoes with mix poder then coat them with bread crumbs.

  • Step3: Carefully drop them into the hot oil immediately after coating.

  • Step4: Use a slotted spoon to remove the tempura pieces from the pan when the color turns to the golden. Then enjoy your meal.

Eight Treasure Rice Pudding

Ingredients

  • 2cups sticky rice
  • 100g Red Bean Paste
  • 1tablespoon Pork fat/Butter
  • Handful of Sugar lotus Seeds
  • Handful of Cranberries
  • Handful of dates
  • Handful of Raisins

Instructions

  • Step1: Steam the sticky rice around 1hours. Mix the rice with pork fat or butter.

  • Step2: spread the topping on the bottom of the bowl(any pattern you like).

  • Step3: Make red bean paste as a ball.

  • Step4: Add a layer of cooked sticky rice then add the red bean paste layer.

  • Step5: Fill up the bowl with the rest of the rice.

  • Step6: Steam the second time around half hours. Then enjoy your meal.
